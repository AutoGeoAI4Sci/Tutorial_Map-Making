## Notes for Maintainers

- If a Zoom link expires or access changes, keep the contributor credit and update the link only after confirming the new share URL.
- Do not upload downloaded Zoom videos to this repository unless the presenter and host explicitly approve redistribution.
- When adding notes derived from a recording, mark them as summaries rather than transcripts unless transcript sharing is approved.
